4. Concentric border-radius (DexCard / StableCard)

Outer card uses rounded-[10px] with 12px padding, but inner chips are rounded-sm (2px). The skill's rule: outer radius ≈ inner radius + padding. Look at the corners — the "after" version's curves track each other.

6. Body text (text-wrap: pretty + font smoothing)

pretty avoids orphan words at paragraph ends. -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased on the root softens text on Mac displays — subtle, but the whole UI feels less "rendered."
